#Buddy OS

### AI-Powered Life Operating System for Students & Young Professionals

> Transforming schedules, goals, opportunities, meetings, and productivity into one intelligent workspace.

Built for **Zuup Hackathon 2026**

---

## Problem Statement

Students and young professionals/ Professors often juggle multiple disconnected tools for managing classes, assignments, exams, projects, meetings, reminders, internships, and career growth.

This fragmented workflow leads to:

* Missed deadlines
* Forgotten exams and meetings
* Poor productivity
* Lost opportunities
* Lack of long-term planning

There is no unified platform that helps users manage both their academic and professional journeys intelligently.

---

## Our Solution

**BuddyOS** is an AI-powered life operating system designed to help users stay organized, productive, and future-focused.

Instead of using multiple apps for planning, reminders, calendars, schedules, and career management, StudentOS provides a single intelligent workspace.

The platform supports both:

**Student Mode** – Academic planning and growth

**Professional Mode** – Work and career management
